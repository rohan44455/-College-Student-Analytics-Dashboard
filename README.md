## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents a **two-page interactive Power BI dashboard** built on a dataset of **2,000 college students** from the **Academic Year 2024–25**. It covers key dimensions of student life — academics, placements, internships, fees, mental health, and demographics.

The dashboard is designed for **college administrators, academic counselors, and policy planners** to identify trends, flag at-risk students, and make informed decisions about support systems.

| Metric | Value |
|---|---|
| 📚 Total Students | 2,000 |
| 🏫 Departments | 15+ |
| 📍 States Covered | 20+ |
| 📅 Academic Year | 2024–25 |
| 📄 Dashboard Pages | 2 |

---

## 🎯 Project Objective

> **To transform raw student data into actionable visual insights that support data-driven decisions in education management.**

The specific goals of this project are:

- 📈 **Track academic performance** (GPA, backlogs, study hours) across departments and years
- 🧠 **Monitor student wellness** through mental health and physical fitness scores
- 💼 **Analyze placement & internship** readiness across different student segments
- 💰 **Understand fee structures** and scholarship impact on student outcomes
- 🌍 **Explore geographic & demographic** diversity across gender, state, and family income
- 🎛️ **Enable slice-and-dice filtering** by department, gender, scholarship type, career goal, year of study, and accommodation

## 💡 Project Insights

### 🏆 Academic
- **MBA and Data Science** departments lead with the **highest average GPA of 8.5**
- **Law, Chemistry, and Political Science** also score well at **8.4 avg GPA**
- GPA shows a **positive correlation with daily study hours** (visible in scatter plot)
- **Year 2 students** carry the **most backlogs**, suggesting a pressure point mid-program

### 💼 Placement & Internship
- Only **20.94%** of students have been placed — indicating room for career support improvement
- **24.93%** internship completion rate — about 1 in 4 students gain internship experience
- A significant chunk (**~25.6%**) have internships as "Not Done" — a concern for employability

### 🌍 Demographics
- Student base is **geographically diverse** — spread across 20+ Indian states
- **Rajasthan, Maharashtra, and Delhi** are the top contributing states
- Gender split: **~48% Male, ~48% Female, ~4% Non-binary** — near parity

### 💰 Fees & Scholarships
- **61.3% of students** receive some form of scholarship — a strong welfare signal
- Average fee paid is **₹83,699** — reflecting partial fee waivers across scholarship recipients

### 🧠 Wellness & Engagement
- Average **Mental Health Score of 7.0/10** suggests moderate-to-good student well-being
- **Library visits peak in Year 3** — suggesting increased research engagement
- **Economics and Electrical Engineering** lead in online course completions

## ✅ Final Conclusion

> This dashboard delivers a **360° view of student life** — from classroom performance to career readiness, from mental wellness to financial support.

**Key takeaways for institutional action:**

1. 🔴 **Placement rate at 20.94%** needs urgent attention — targeted career coaching programs recommended
2. 🟡 **Year 2 backlogs are highest** — early academic intervention (tutoring, counseling) during Year 2 is critical
3. 🟢 **GPA is healthy at 8.33** overall, with standout performance in MBA and Data Science
4. 🟢 **Scholarship coverage at 61.3%** is commendable and appears to support student retention
5. 🟡 **Mental health score of 7/10** is decent, but the 30% below average warrants proactive wellness initiatives
6. 🔵 **Internship completion at ~25%** should be doubled through industry partnerships and mandatory internship policies

The dashboard empowers **data-driven decision-making** across academic, financial, and wellness domains — making it a valuable tool for college leadership.
